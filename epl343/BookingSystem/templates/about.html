A Few Words about me

MUDr. Stelios Theodorou obtained his General Medicine degree from the First Faculty of Medicine, Charles University Prague in 2022, where he excelled. Throughout his years he demonstrated special interest in Endocrinology Specialty and Medical Student Education and Well Being. He observed Endocrinology in NHS Plymouth University Hospitals and Endocrinology Outpatient clinics in the Faculty Polyclinic of the General University Hospital in Prague. He served as a teaching assistant for Anatomy and Physiology in his University as well as actively participated in the Student Association of his Medical Faculty as a Student Guide and Vice President of Activities and Media. Prior to that, he graduated from American Academy Larnaca in 2013. There he followed the GCSE and GCE curriculum for subjects like Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics and more. After high school, he graduated from Artillery Officer University in 2013 in Greece and served as a ranking officer between 2013 and 2015 in Cyprus National Guard. For his medical experience he attended various extracurricular internships, attended various medical seminars and published various research papers.
